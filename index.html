Features

A Better Way to Manage Your Critical Information

SecureQR Life provides encrypted storage and controlled access to your most important documents.

Document Storage

Securely store digital copies of IDs, licenses, marksheets, and address proofs in one place, accessible only via your QR.

Health Monitoring

Update and track your medical records like allergies, blood sugar levels, and emergency contacts for hospitals.

Password Protection

Multi-layer security with personal passwords and organization verification for sensitive information access.

Authorized Access

Only schools, hospitals, police stations can decode receiver QRs, ensuring information only reaches intended recipients.

Vehicle Application

Display your QR on windshield or mobile for emergency identification during accidents or roadside checks.

Secure Printing

Share documents through authorized print shops using receiver QR codes to prevent misuse of your copies.
